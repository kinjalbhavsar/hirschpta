Volunteer

There is a role for everyone — from one hour a year to leading a program all semester. Your time makes Hirsch exceptional.

Why Volunteer

Your Time Matters

🎯
Make a Direct Impact

Volunteer hours go straight into student programs — every minute you give enriches a child's school experience.

🤝
Build Community

Meet other Hirsch families, form lasting friendships, and become an active part of the school community.

⭐
Support Your Child

Kids thrive when they see their parents engaged at school. Volunteering shows them that their education is a priority.
