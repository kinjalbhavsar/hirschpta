Our Programs

15+ programs funded and coordinated by the Hirsch PTA, enriching students in arts, academics, athletics, and culture.

Arts & Expression
🎵 Arts
Music for Minors II

Weekly 30-minute music classes for TK–2nd grade students, bringing live music instruction directly into the classroom through trained volunteers.

🎨 Arts
F.A.M.E. Program

Fine Arts & Music Education — an integrated program enriching students through visual arts, performing arts, and hands-on creative experiences.

🌟 Arts
Reflections

The National PTA Reflections art competition invites students to create original works in visual arts, literature, music, photography, film, and dance around an annual theme.

🎗 Arts
Talent Show

An annual showcase where Hirsch students perform original acts — singing, dancing, magic, comedy, and more — in front of their families and peers.

Academic Enrichment
🎤 Academic
Educational Assemblies

PTA-funded guest speakers and performers bring curriculum-aligned, engaging presentations on science, history, social-emotional learning, and more.

📚 Academic
Debate Club

Students develop critical thinking, public speaking, and research skills through structured debate practice and friendly interschool competitions.

🎓 Academic
Scholarships

Merit-based scholarships recognizing academic achievement and community service, helping Hirsch graduates transition to middle school and beyond.

Athletics & Health
🏈 Athletics
Basketball Program

Intramural basketball for Hirsch students, emphasizing teamwork, sportsmanship, and physical fitness in a fun and inclusive environment.

🏃 Athletics
Field Day

An end-of-year celebration of fitness and fun — students compete in relay races, obstacle courses, and team games on Hirsch's field.

🥂 Athletics
Red Ribbon Week

A national awareness campaign where students pledge to live drug-free lives, featuring fun themed dress days, assemblies, and classroom activities.

Community & Culture
🏭 Community
Harvest Festival

A beloved fall tradition with games, food, music, and community fun. Families come together to celebrate the season and support Hirsch.

🌐 Community
Multicultural Week

Celebrating Hirsch's rich diversity — students, families, and staff share food, music, dance, and stories from cultures around the world.

🥡 Community
Thanksgiving Luncheon

A special Thanksgiving meal bringing students, teachers, and families together for a warm community celebration before the holiday break.

🎬 Community
Family Movie Nights

Outdoor movie screenings on the school blacktop — bring a blanket, grab some snacks, and enjoy a film with the whole Hirsch community.

🏆 Community
Teacher Appreciation Week

A week dedicated to showing our amazing Hirsch teachers and staff how much they're valued — with treats, lunches, and heartfelt thank-yous.

Help Make These Programs Happen

Every program is run by parent volunteers. Whether you have an hour or a full semester, there's a role for you at Hirsch.
