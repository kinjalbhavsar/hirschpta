What We Do

Enriching Hirsch, one program at a time

The Hirsch PTA is a parent-run, 501(c)(3) nonprofit committed to building a stronger community among Hirsch families. Every dollar we raise stays at Hirsch — funding classroom supplies, field trips, arts programs, cultural events, and more.

Membership is open to all parents, guardians, teachers, and community members. There is no time or volunteer commitment required to be a member — your dues alone make a direct impact.
